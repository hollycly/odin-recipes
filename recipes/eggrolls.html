Pork Egg Rolls

Description

Khmer egg rolls are the best. We used to make these all the time growing up. This is a fusion of the recipe I learned from my mom, and the recipe from Peter Duong.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup shaved taro
  • 1 cup shaved jicama
  • 3-4 shaved carrots
  • 6 oz cellophane noodles
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 package egg roll wrappers
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add your ground pork, taro, jicama, carrots, noodles, and black pepper. Give it a nice mix.
  2. In a small bowl, separate your egg and keep only the yolk to seal the egg roll.
  3. Take a single egg roll wrapper sheet and place diagonally so the corner is right in front of you.
  4. Now, let's roll! Take 1-2 tbsp of filling and place on the corner of the egg roll wrapper sheet.
  5. Tuck in the bottom, tuck in the left corner, tuck in the right corner, and roll straight up. Seal it with your egg wash.
  6. Fry your egg rolls until a beautiful crispy golden brown in neutral oil. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce, and enjoy!
