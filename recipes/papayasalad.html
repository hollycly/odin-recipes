Papaya Salad

Description

“Papaya salad is a staple in Southeast Asian cuisine. The crunch from the unripe papaya soaked in a spicy umami dressing makes your mouth water. Many Southeast Asian countries have their variations but this one is a balance of tangy, sweet, and spicy. In Khmer we call it ‘bok lahong’ which translates to smashing papaya.” - Peter Duong

Ingredients

  • 1 green unripe papaya
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 carrot (optional)
  • 1 salted crab (preserved in a jar)
  • 1-2 limes

Sauce Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 5 Thai chili
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp fish paste
  • 1 tsp dry fish paste

Steps

  1. Prep your papaya and carrots with a shredder. Slice your lime and tomatoes.
  2. In a mortar start out by smashing your garlic and Thai chilis.
  3. Add the remainder of your “sauce ingredients” to the mortar, pound it down and give it a nice mix.
  4. Add in your lime and tomatoes, give it a smash.
  5. Finish your papaya salad by add crabs, mix, and enjoy!
